# Style Presets

Choose one preset by default, or create a custom blend when the article needs it.

## 1. Warm Professional Knowledge

- Good for: beginner tutorials, self-media growth, psychology-informed content
- Mood: trustworthy, approachable, calm
- Palette: warm off-white `#F7F1E8`, charcoal `#2B2B2B`, muted orange `#D98B4A`, soft green `#7C9A7A`
- Visuals: desk scenes, cards, flow arrows, gentle human figures, soft shadows
- Avoid: black-heavy cyber style, neon overload, aggressive contrast

## 2. Clean Workflow Tutorial

- Good for: AI tools, operation steps, productivity workflows
- Mood: clear, practical, low-friction
- Palette: white `#FFFFFF`, light gray `#F2F4F7`, blue `#3B82F6`, green `#22A06B`, dark text `#1F2937`
- Visuals: process maps, numbered cards, interface-like panels, before/after
- Avoid: complex backgrounds, decorative clutter

## 3. Psychology Popular Science

- Good for: psychology, emotion, growth, relationship, cognition
- Mood: soft, insightful, human
- Palette: cream `#FAF3E8`, rose `#D98BA0`, sage `#8FAF8F`, brown-gray `#5B504A`
- Visuals: simple human scenes, symbolic objects, mind maps, soft illustration
- Avoid: medical coldness, dramatic sadness, clinical diagrams unless article requires it

## 4. Sharp Opinion Contrast

- Good for: strong viewpoints, myth-busting, contrarian essays
- Mood: crisp, memorable, slightly tense
- Palette: off-white `#F8F7F2`, black `#111111`, red `#D64545`, yellow `#F2C94C`
- Visuals: split screen, comparison blocks, visual conflict, bold labels
- Avoid: too many colors, cute elements that weaken the argument

## 5. Magazine Editorial

- Good for: mature reflections, public account essays, personal brand articles
- Mood: polished, editorial, thoughtful
- Palette: warm white `#F6F1EA`, ink `#222222`, muted blue `#536878`, copper `#B36B45`
- Visuals: cinematic scenes, refined typography, restrained graphic overlays
- Avoid: dense text blocks, childish stickers

## 6. Light Tech Productivity

- Good for: AI workflow, automation, agent tools, knowledge work
- Mood: modern, efficient, but not distant
- Palette: light background `#F6F8FB`, blue `#2563EB`, cyan `#06B6D4`, graphite `#1E293B`, lime accent `#A3E635`
- Visuals: nodes, flow lines, clean dashboards, abstract tool chains
- Avoid: dark sci-fi, purple-blue gradient dominance, hard-to-read neon

## Customization Rules

- If the article targets beginners, prefer warmth and clarity over visual spectacle.
- If the user asks for "高级", do not automatically choose dark tech style; make it refined through typography, spacing, and restrained color.
- If using reference images, let the reference style override these presets unless it conflicts with readability.
